Tutorial-scoped mstransform-style MeasurementSet materialization.

CASA routes mstransform through the mstransformer tool and a chain of TVI layers. This module implements the IRC+10216 tutorial subset needed by downstream line-imaging workflows: row selection plus per-SPW channel selection into a new on-disk MeasurementSet while preserving the standard subtables and updating spectral-window channel metadata.
