Expand description

A region within a lattice defining a subset of pixels.

Corresponds to the C++ LCRegion abstract base class. Regions describe which pixels of a lattice are “inside” and which are “outside” via a boolean mask. The region is bounded by a rectangular bounding box.

Concrete implementations include LCBox, LCEllipsoid, and set-algebra combinators.
