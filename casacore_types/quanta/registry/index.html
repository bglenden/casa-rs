Unit registry: lookup, caching, and user-defined units.

UnitRegistry holds all known unit definitions and provides the lookup that the parser uses to resolve unit names. It is the Rust counterpart of C++ casa::UnitMap.

The global registry is initialised once from the built-in tables in registry_data and extended at runtime via UnitRegistry::put_user and UnitRegistry::remove_user.
