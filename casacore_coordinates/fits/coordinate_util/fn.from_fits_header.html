Parses a FitsHeader (and image shape) into a CoordinateSystem.

The shape slice gives the expected axis lengths (used for Stokes axis decoding). It must have the same number of entries as NAXIS in the header.

§Axis identification

Each axis is identified by its CTYPEn keyword:

  • RA--, DEC-, GLON, GLAT, SLON, SLAT with a projection suffix become a DirectionCoordinate.
  • FREQ or VELO becomes a SpectralCoordinate.
  • STOKES becomes a StokesCoordinate.
  • Anything else becomes a LinearCoordinate.

§Errors

Returns CoordinateError if required keywords are missing or if an unsupported projection is encountered.
