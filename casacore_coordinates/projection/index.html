Spherical projection types and mathematics.

This module implements the map projections used to relate the native spherical coordinates (phi, theta) on the celestial sphere to the intermediate (x, y) coordinates in the projection plane. The projection math follows Calabretta & Greisen (2002), “Representations of celestial coordinates in FITS” (A&A 395, 1077-1122).
